    The Brewery Everyone Is Talking About In Your State

    Go straight to the source of your favorite suds.

    By Hannah Doolin
    Apr 7, 2017
    Skip Ad
    Previous

    view gallery
    52 Photos

    Sometimes grabbing a cold one at your local sports bar just won't cut it. Instead, go straight to the source and have a drink at one of the best breweries in America to sample endless varieties of beer on tap and peek behind the scenes of the beer-making process on a brewery tour. According to Foursquare City Guide, these spots are loved for their tasting rooms, killer food menus, live entertainment, take-home growlers and beyond.

    Alabama

    Good People Brewing Company, Birmingham

    "Some of the best beers in Birmingham. The newly renovated taproom is amazing, with an outdoor area. This place is a MUST before Barons games, with Regions Field just across the street." - Foursquare user Jonathan H.

    Foursquare/Matt H.
    4 Of 52

    Arkansas

    Lost Forty Brewing, Little Rock

    "Excellent atmosphere, great food and even better beer. The pimento cheese is a must order. Love the honey bock. Great place to have a few beers and watch a game." - Foursquare user Justin Myers

    Foursquare/Lost Forty Brewery

    Arizona

    Mother Road Brewing Company, Flagstaff

    "What a great find! Very friendly bartender, nice interior with a hipsterish vibe and, most importantly, great homebrewed beer!" - Foursquare user Olga Isaeva

    Foursquare/Dan P.
    6 Of 52

    California

    AleSmith Brewing Company, San Diego

    "Great place to taste seasonal and special editions. They also offer tours if you are interested. Food trucks outside. Friendly staff." - Foursquare user Ricardo Hernandez

    Foursquare/Alex
    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below
    7 Of 52

    Colorado

    Odell Brewing Company, Fort Collins

    "Excellent, excellent brewery! Friendly staff, great beer, and a beautiful outdoor space. The fire pit is warm in the winter and there is live music on the outdoor stage on Sundays in the summer." - Foursquare user Tracy Marcello

    Foursquare/Odell Brewing Company
    8 Of 52

    Connecticut

    Two Roads Brewing Company, Stratford

    "One of my new favorite breweries. Delicious beer, food trucks outside, and they'll even send a bus to pick you up from the train station." - Foursquare user Cameron Gidari

    Foursquare/Ryan R.

    9 Of 52

    Washington D.C.

    DC Brau Brewing Co, Washington D.C.

    Took a tour and learned a lot about canning beer that I didn't know before. They have a great double IPA. Get a flight to try a bunch while you're there." - Foursquare user Liz Evans

    Foursquare/Matt W.

    Delaware

    Dogfish Head Craft Brewery, Milton

    "They treat you like kings and queens. Four free beer samples before a free tour, plus if you are lucky enough you'll get to see the owner, Sam Calagione. Did I mention they have free wifi too?" - Foursquare user Zeebamehrin Manavi
